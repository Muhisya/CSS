Milkshake

A milkshake is a sweet, creamy, and indulgent beverage made with a combination of milk, ice cream, and flavorings or sweeteners. It's typically thick and velvety in texture, with a rich and indulgent taste that's both refreshing and satisfying.

Smoothie

A smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious blended drink made from a combination of fruits, yogurt, milk, or other liquid ingredients. It's typically smooth and creamy in texture, with a vibrant and appealing color that reflects the ingredients used

Soda

Soda, also known as pop or soft drink, is a sweet, fizzy, and refreshing beverage that comes in a wide range of flavors and varieties. It's typically carbonated, with a lively effervescence that tickles the nose and tantalizes the taste buds.
